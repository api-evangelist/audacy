PHOENIX, AZ – June 16, 2026 – Audacy elevates Ben “Complex” Romero as Brand Manager of Live 101.5 (KALV-FM) in Phoenix. In this role, he will oversee the station’s content strategy, talent, operations and branding. Complex will continue to serve as the Brand Manager for 106.5 The End (KUDL-FM) and 102.5 KSFM (KSFM-FM) in Sacramento.
