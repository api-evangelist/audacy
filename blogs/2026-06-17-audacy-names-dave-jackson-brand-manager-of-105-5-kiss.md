Audacy named Dave Jackson Brand Manager of 105.5 KISS FM (WKXJ-FM) in Chattanooga, overseeing content strategy, talent, operations and branding. Jackson will continue as Brand Manager for B93.7 (WFBC-FM), The Block (WFBC-HD2) and Magic Upstate (WFBC-HD4) in Greenville; he started his radio career in 1999 and rejoined Audacy in 2014.
