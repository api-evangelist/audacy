Audacy elevated Ben "Complex" Romero to Brand Manager of Live 101.5 (KALV-FM) in Phoenix, overseeing the station's content strategy, talent, operations and branding. A radio programmer with over 20 years of experience, Complex will continue managing 106.5 The End (KUDL-FM) and 102.5 KSFM in Sacramento, and previously spent 11 years programming KKFR "Power 98.3 / 96.1" in Phoenix.
