99.9 KISW (KISW-FM), an Audacy station in Seattle, has announced a partnership with Washington State Ferries (WSF) to celebrate the state’s marine highway system’s 75th anniversary. On May 29, the station will host a historic, first-of-its-kind four-hour live broadcast from aboard the Edmonds-Kingston ferry. From 6:00 a.m.
