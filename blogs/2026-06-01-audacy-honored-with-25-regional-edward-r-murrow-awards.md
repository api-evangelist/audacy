Audacy was honored with 25 Regional Edward R. Murrow Awards, presented by the Radio Television Digital News Association (RTDNA), for outstanding achievements in broadcast and electronic journalism. Four Audacy brands were awarded the coveted Overall Excellence award in their respective regions.
